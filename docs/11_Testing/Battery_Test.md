# Battery Capacity & BMS Test

## Purpose
This document details the test plan to verify battery capacity, discharge curves, and BMS safety cut-offs.

## Test Procedure
1.  **Capacity Test**: Discharge the battery pack from $12.6	ext{V}$ to $9.0	ext{V}$ under a constant 3.4A load, verifying that capacity meets the 6800 mAh specification.
2.  **BMS Cut-off Verification**: Under a controlled discharge, verify that the BMS cuts power when any cell drops below $2.8	ext{V}$.
