# Mechanical Frame Assembly Guide

## Purpose
This document provides a step-by-step assembly guide for the PRAYAS V1 robot frame.

## Required Fasteners
*   **M5 Bolts (30mm)**: For securing the PVC floor flange to the plywood base.
*   **M4 Machine Screws (15mm)**: For attaching motor brackets to the plywood deck.
*   **M3 Bolts & Brass Inserts**: For assembling 3D-printed joints and servo brackets.

## Step-by-Step Assembly Steps

### Step 1: Base Deck Assembly
1.  Align the 4 Johnson motor steel brackets on the underside of the 12mm plywood base plate.
2.  Secure the brackets using M4 screws and washers.
3.  Mount the 4 geared motors into the brackets and tighten the set screws.
4.  Attach the 100mm rubber wheels to the motor shafts.

### Step 2: Torso Spine Installation
1.  Center the 4-inch PVC floor flange on the top side of the plywood base plate.
2.  Drill 4 holes through the plywood using the flange as a template.
3.  Secure the flange to the base deck using M5 bolts, washers, and locknuts.
4.  Apply PVC cement to the end of the 70 cm PVC pipe and insert it into the flange.

### Step 3: Torso and Limb Integration
1.  Route the power and signal cables through the inside of the PVC pipe column.
2.  Mount the 3D-printed shoulder adapter onto the top of the PVC pipe and secure it with two through-bolts.
3.  Assemble the 3D-printed arm segments, inserting the MG995 servos into their brackets.
4.  Attach the arms to the shoulder frame.
5.  Install the neck servo and mount the head assembly on top of the shoulder frame.
