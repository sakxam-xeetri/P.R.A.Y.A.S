# Weight Distribution Analysis

## Purpose
This document details the weight distribution across the PRAYAS V1 subassemblies, ensuring balance and structural stability.

## Mass Budgets
The estimated masses of the robot's components are as follows:

| Subassembly | Material / Components | Estimated Mass (kg) | Percentage of Total |
| :--- | :--- | :---: | :---: |
| **Lower Base** | 12mm Plywood, steel brackets, shroud | 1.8 kg | 25.7% |
| **Drivetrain** | 4 Johnson motors, 4 rubber wheels | 1.5 kg | 21.4% |
| **Torso Spine**| 4-inch PVC pipe (70cm length) | 1.0 kg | 14.3% |
| **Upper Torso**| 3D Printed panels and electronics | 1.2 kg | 17.1% |
| **Limb Servos**| 7 × MG995 servos and mounts | 0.5 kg | 7.1% |
| **Battery** | 3S2P Lithium cell pack | 0.5 kg | 7.1% |
| **Electronics**| Wiring, regulators, sensors | 0.5 kg | 7.1% |
| **Total** | | **7.0 kg** | **100%** |

## Design Recommendations
To ensure stability, the heaviest components (the battery and motor drivers) are mounted directly to the plywood base plate. This design keeps the center of gravity low and prevents tipping during acceleration.
