# Periodic Maintenance Schedule

## Purpose
This document outlines the maintenance tasks and intervals to ensure the long-term reliability of the robot.

## Maintenance Tasks
*   **Every 10 Operating Hours**: Inspect motor mounts and frame fasteners, tightening any loose screws.
*   **Every 50 Operating Hours**: Inspect the wheels for wear. Check joint play and grease the servo gears if necessary.
*   **Every 100 Operating Hours**: Check the battery pack for signs of swelling. Clean dust from the electronics bay and ensure fans are running smoothly.
