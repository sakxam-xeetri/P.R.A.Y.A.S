# Future Manufacturing Upgrades

## Purpose
This document details potential manufacturing upgrades to improve the durability and appearance of future robot versions.

## Proposed Upgrades
*   **Injection-molded or vacuum-formed casing**: Replace the sunboard covers with vacuum-formed ABS plastic shells to improve durability and appearance.
*   **Aluminum Torso Column**: Replace the PVC pipe column with an anodized aluminum tube to increase rigidity.
*   **Serial Bus Servos**: Upgrade the analog MG995 servos to serial bus smart servos (e.g. Lewansoul/Feetech STS series) to allow position and temperature feedback.
