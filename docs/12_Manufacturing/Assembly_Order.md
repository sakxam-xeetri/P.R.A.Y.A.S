# Step-by-Step Frame Assembly Sequence

## Purpose
This document details the recommended assembly sequence for the PRAYAS V1 robot frame.

## Assembly Steps
1.  **Base Assembly**: Mount the motors and drivers to the plywood deck. Install the wheels.
2.  **Torso Column Installation**: Bolt the PVC flange to the base, insert the PVC column, and secure the wiring harness inside the pipe.
3.  **Upper Torso Installation**: Mount the shoulder adapter, upper torso panels, and electronics boards.
4.  **Arm Assembly**: Mount the servos into the arm brackets and attach the arm segments to the shoulder joints.
5.  **Head Integration**: Mount the neck servo and head shell. Route cables to the electronics bay.
