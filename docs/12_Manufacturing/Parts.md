# Custom Fabricated Parts List

## Purpose
This document lists the custom parts that must be fabricated to assemble the PRAYAS V1 robot.

## Custom Fabricated Parts
*   **Plywood Base Deck**: Rectangular base plate.
*   **Torso Spine PVC Pipe**: Structural column.
*   **3D Printed Shoulder Adapter**: Mounts the upper torso to the PVC pipe.
*   **3D Printed Upper Torso Panels**: Houses the head servo, electronics, and arm mounts.
*   **3D Printed Arm Segments**: Custom brackets for the MG995 servos.
*   **Sunboard Base Cover**: Shroud panels that attach to the plywood deck to cover the motors and battery.
