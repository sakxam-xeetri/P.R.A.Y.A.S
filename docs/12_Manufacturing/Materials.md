# Raw Materials Machining

## Purpose
This document details the preparation, cutting, and drilling instructions for raw materials like plywood and PVC.

## Fabrication Steps
1.  **Plywood Base Plate**: Cut a $400	ext{ mm} 	imes 350	ext{ mm}$ rectangle from a sheet of 12mm birch plywood. Drill holes for motor mounts, routing brackets, and the PVC floor flange.
2.  **Torso Spine**: Cut a 70cm length of 4-inch Schedule 40 PVC pipe. Drill 20mm routing holes near the top and bottom of the pipe to allow routing signal and power cables.
