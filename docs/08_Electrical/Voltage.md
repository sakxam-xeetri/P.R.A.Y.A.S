# Voltage Regulation & Levels

## Purpose
This document outlines the voltage levels and the characteristics of the regulators used in PRAYAS V1.

## Regulator Specifications

### 1. 6.0V Servo Regulator (SZBK07)
*   **Type**: Synchronous Step-Down Buck Converter.
*   **Input Voltage Range**: 9V to 40V.
*   **Output Voltage**: 6.0V (adjusted via onboard potentiometer).
*   **Maximum Continuous Current**: 12A (15A peak with forced air cooling).
*   **Efficiency**: ~94%.

### 2. 5.0V Logic Regulator (LM2596)
*   **Type**: Step-Down Switching Regulator.
*   **Input Voltage Range**: 7V to 35V.
*   **Output Voltage**: 5.0V (fixed or adjusted).
*   **Maximum Continuous Current**: 3A.
*   **Efficiency**: ~80%.
